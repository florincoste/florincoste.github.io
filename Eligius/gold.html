Gold Service

  • Full system inspection including:
  • Check high & low pressure switch operation
  • Check operation of compressor & oil level
  • Check all evaporator & condenser fans are working
  • Test thermostat is operating correctly, including the operation of switches

  • Check drive belts
  • Test run system
